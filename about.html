About Us

THE LIMURU JOINT UNIVERSITY STUDENTS ASSOCIATION (LIJUSA)

Background

Limuru Joint University Students Association herein abbreviated as LIJUSA, is a Community Based Organization (CBO) registered in Kenya in the commissioner of social services in the Ministry of Gender, Sports, Culture and Social Services. The association was founded in the year 2008 by a group of University students hailing from Limuru Constituency with the aim of mobilizing the youth in the institutions of higher learning to give back to the community, some of the founders includes John Mukabi, Dan Mburu, George Kiambuthi amongst others. The mission was, and still is, to tap into the intellectual capacity of the members, impart them with social, leadership and advocacy skills that are instrumental in creating a holistic community.

Objectives

  • To promote unity and harmony among students in institutions of higher learning residing in Limuru Constituency.
  • To empower the youth by creating a safe space where they can exercise their rights and freedoms of conscience, expression, association and belief as enshrined in the Bill of Rights without any form of discrimination or intimidation.
  • To create a positive relationship between the students and the community in order to transform the community and stimulate growth and development in Limuru Constituency.
  • Capacity building among the youth and the community at large through regular mentorship programs and workshops that seek to nature the members in professional, interpersonal and social skills, advocacy and transformative leadership.
  • To promote national values and principles of governance in the community in a bid to create patriotic and responsible citizens.
  • To promote the quality of education within the Constituency through regular mentorship programs to schools within the Limuru Constituency and empowerment of the youth.
  • Giving back to the society through charity works and welfare programs.
  • Networking and forging partnerships with various public and private organizations to steer ahead our goals and objectives for the good of our community.

Membership

Membership of LIJUSA comprises of general members, associates, corporate members and affiliate members, all of whom are subject to the Constitution of LIJUSA. All members are admitted into the Association after having met the qualifications outlined in the Constitution of LIJUSA and upon payment for a subscription fee which is renewable annually. Moreover, there is created the position of a patron who is the chief advisor of the Association and a link between LIJUSA and the public. The patron is appointed by the Executive with the approval of the members of the Association.

Leadership

LIJUSA is governed by the executive committee in collaboration with the associates. The executive is made up of nine members headed by the Chairman, all of whom are duly elected by the members of the Association. LIJUSA has a 5 Ministerial dockets; Health, Environment, ICT, Education and Leadership which are tasked with the mandate to spearhead the development agendas of the association and are governed by the respective Cabinet Secretaries with the assistance of Principal Secretaries who are appointed by the Executive with the approval of the members of the Association.
